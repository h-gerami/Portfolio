How I approach customer issues

  • Intake: clarify expected vs. actual behavior, capture logs, and identify regression vs. configuration.
  • Reproduce: create a minimal failing case; add unit/integration tests to lock in the fix.
  • Fix & harden: improve error messages, add retry/idempotency where relevant, and update observability.
  • Document: update runbooks and customer‑facing guides; share learnings with the team.

Outcome: faster resolutions, fewer repeat tickets, and clearer insight into system health.
